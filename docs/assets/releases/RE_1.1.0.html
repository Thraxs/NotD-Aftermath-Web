General Theme of Change

  • Gameplay changes
  • New models / skins
  • Bug fixes
  • Changes missed from 1.6 and earlier

Changes

Gameplay changes

  • Unknown entities spawned from Prometheus corpse are now timed life. Parasites are still the same.
  • Added new watchtower in the SW entrance of the airfield. Slightly adjusted pathing for all towers.
  • Added FN Mk17 (SCAR-H) battle rifle for Rifleman/Infantry Unit Leader.
  • Added unit name for extinguished Ground Illumination Flare dummy.
  • The "-team" command now displays the classes for KIA marines.
  • ALICE will now display a message to all players when the M242 is disassembled.
  • Added Hell difficulty (very early work in progress)
  • You can no longer get a fracture while jumping (to prevent instant death)
  • Updated Containment Titan dropsite ping duration. From 2 minutes to 10/4/3 based on difficulty (Normal/Nightmare/Hell).
  • Increased the size and changed color of type A parasites (aka super sites).
  • Extra 50 XP to all Marines when all 16 civilians make it through the Convoy Mission. 75 XP on Nightmare. 100xp on Hell.
  • [Infantry Unit Leader]: Switched rifle grenade hotkey back to "E". Hotkey for defensive stance is now "S".
  • [Delta Operator]: Switched rifle grenade hotkey back to "E".

New models / skins

  • Added new icon for Forward Observer.
  • Added new icon for Assaultman.
  • Added new icon for Infantry Unit Leader.
  • Updated icon for FLIR sector scan.
  • Updated texture for all FelHound based units.
  • Updated texture for all Ghoul based units.
  • Updated texture for all Gargoyle based units.
  • Updated texture for Eris.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed spelling of Field Marshal.
  • Fixed Light Weapons Training showing as a normal ability for Rifleman.
  • Fixed Ammunition Feed being triggered repeatedly on use.
  • Fixed text spacing on save files.
  • Orbital Artillery shock is no longer permanent.
  • Refactored weather effect caching. This should fix effects like BHD wind not disappearing after a while like they are supposed to.
  • The "Attack Formation" sound will no longer play for the Lyganesh F-22s after it has been called off.
  • Fixed positioning of Containment helicopter minis.
  • Undo changes to MK-2 nano processor to try fixing the stuck CE bug.

Changes missed from 1.6a and earlier

  • Agents damage type changed to Creature type damage and their feedback reduced from 48 to 36 per hit.
  • Removed Snipers from LC entirely. MM's are still present however.
  • Reduced range and increased cooldown of IUL FM.
  • Recalculated the DPS boost on TX1
  • Slight garg damage reduction.
  • Reduced Necrotroph's duration from 20 minutes to 10 minutes.
  • Reduced Fracture's duration from 20 minutes to 10 minutes.
  • Removed Heavy Carapace type armor from all parasites and made them standard Carapace.
  • Mini FT Flame Burst mana cost reduced.
  • Saline IV mana cost now 35 at all lvls rather than increasing with lvls.
  • Field aid mana cost halved.
  • Field aid casting time increased by 50%.
  • Reduced Infrared Strobe mana cost from 30 to 15.
  • Slightly lowered Thanatos' attack buffer range.
  • Increased Field Light truesight range.
  • Give the IUL and the Rifleman a slightly different Critical Strike skill.
  • Rifle Grenade cooldown increased at lower lvls.
  • Rifle Grenade mana cost set to 30 at all levels.
  • Rifle Grenade tooltip fixed
  • Rifle Grenade skill buffed from 800/1100/1400/1700/2000 to 1300/1550/1800/2050/2300.
  • Diablo chance set from 1.3% to 2% chance to spawn for low ranked squads.
  • Diablo chance set from 5% chance to 10% chance to spawn for mid (40k average XP) ranked squads.
  • Fixed a problem with diablo chance to spawn during Dark Clouds for mid ranked squads.
  • Fire Mission base damage updated from 300 to 400.
  • Com tower set to invul after Delta AF.
  • Updated SAM-R damage for marksman units from 75 to 100.
  • Golf Companies Claymores can now be attacked like any ordinary unit (if you detect them that is).
  • [Rifleman]: Rifleman has his knife back, he still has a colt though.
  • [Assaultman]: Reduced Assaultman plant chain explosive cooldown to 60 seconds.
